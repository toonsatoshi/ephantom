# EPHANTOM Protocol

## Introduction
The EPHANTOM Protocol is a **Decentralized Autonomous Music Label (DAML)** that enables the collaborative creation and commercial distribution of "Instanced Identities" (IIs). These are virtual music artists whose aesthetic direction, track selection, and brand evolution are governed by a decentralized crowd of curators.

By integrating AI-assisted synthesis with a reputation-weighted consensus model, the protocol produces high-fidelity, market-ready music. Human taste acts as the ultimate filter, determining what "sounds good" while AI serves as a powerful tool for emergent creativity.

## The DAO
EPHANTOM operates as a meritocracy where influence is earned through consistent alignment with aesthetic quality and market success. 

**Note:** The protocol is in its genesis phase. Anyone else who joins at this stage would be the first other person (besides the founder) to be a part of this DAO.
